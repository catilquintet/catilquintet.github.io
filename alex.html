Alex Ho

Flute

Alex is a flute and piccolo player from Hong Kong. He earned a first class Bachelor's degree with honours from Hong Kong Baptist University and a Master of Performance from Guildhall School of Music and Drama. He has excelled in international competitions as a soloist and has performed at Carnegie Hall, Barbican Hall and the Hong Kong Cultural Centre. He freelances with Macau Symphony Orchestra on piccolo.
